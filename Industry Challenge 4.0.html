2017 South Asian Games

The 2017 Southeast Asian Games (Malay: Sukan Asia Tenggara 2017), officially known as the 29th Southeast Asian Games (or simply 29th SEA Games; Malay: Sukan Asia Tenggara ke-29 or Sukan SEA ke-29) and commonly known as Kuala Lumpur 2017is a regional multi-sport event that will take place in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, from 19 to 30 August 2017,with 404 events in 38 sports to be featured in the games.


It will be the sixth time that the Southeast Asian Games hel in Malaysia 1965, 1971, 1977, 1989 and 2001 Games and its first time since 2001.


By the time the country host the games in 2017, it will be 16 years after Malaysia last hosted the biennial event. the game closing ceremony will coincide with Malaysia's 60th Independence Day.
